About

We were founded in 2011 to provide a fine arts opurtunity to campers entering 3rd to 8th grade. Performing for a Purpose is a non-for-profit which benefits Catholic Relief Services.
The camp is a week long (July 9-13th) held at St. Monica Parish in the Gym and Cafeteria. Camp begins at noon, immediately following Vacation Bible School and providing a fun full day option for campers.

During the week campers:
  • memorize lines
  • play acting games
  • grow as performers
  • create tee-shirt costumes
  • paint sets
  • eat and make snack
  • sing and dance
